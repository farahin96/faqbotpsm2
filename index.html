Know About FAQBot

Introduction

This website is to get some feedback from users on using a chatbot. This project is the final year project for Computer Science student. This project is about a development of a chatbot on academic programmes offered by the Department of Intelligent Computing and Analytics (ICA), FTMK, Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka (UTeM). The ICA department offers an undergraduate course which is Bachelor of Computer Science in Artificial Intelligence (BITI). The developed chatbot is called FAQBot that is aimed to answer all questions about BITI.

 

What is chatbot?

What is FAQBot?

Faqbot is a chatbot that will communicate with users about ICA Academic Programmes. This FAQBot will be available for all users that visit ICA websites and engage with the conversation. Users will be able to have a conversation and make a queries with chatbot in the websites. The location of the chatbot will be locate at bottom right corner of the websites.

 

Manual to use

Click the blue button on the bottom right corner of this websites. Try to ask any quetion related to ICA Program. Please leave your feedback about this FAQBot, If you think this FAQBot needs improvement, please suggest them.
